Meet Gary Blue

Gary Blue grew up in a small town in Newfoundland, he always enjoyed cooking for his family and perfecting recipes. Gary graduated with honours from culinary school in 1985 and vowed to put his recipes on a menu for other Newfoundlanders to try. He now owns 6 Gary Blue's Diner locations all across the beautiful province that he grew up in!

Our Ingredients...

Are fresh, carefully selected and grown in our beautiful country, Canada. We pride ourselves on quality ingredients so that our customers get what they deserve; the best!

Our Staff...

Are welcoming, friendly and devoted to making your experience the best that it possibly can be. When you walk into a Gary Blue's Diner, you'll be greeted with a warm smile and fantastic service!
